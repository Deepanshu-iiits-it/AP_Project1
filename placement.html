Placement Record 2019

Indian Institute of Information Technology Sonepat witnessed another good placement season for the Batch 2015-19 with approximately 82% students getting placed. 22 companies participated in the recruitment drive including top MNCs. Recognizing IIIT Sonepat competency in the IT sector, reputed MNCs like Adobe System, IBM, Q3 Technology, Posist Technology etc. offered coveted job profiles. Other companies like Osmosys Software Solution Pvt. Ltd., Thought Focus, Ebizon Netinfo, Maq Software, Paytm, Oyo Rooms, Ion Trading, Pine Lab, Loconav, Fundwave, VNL, United Airlines Avis & Solution Pvt. Ltd., Blue Optima, Logic Fruit, Carwale, Gemini Solutions, Jugnoo also made their presence by offering jobs to students from IIIT Sonepat. The average annual package of the batch is Rs. 8.19 Lakh as per the latest record. The highest package offered was INR 40.63 Lakh per annum.
